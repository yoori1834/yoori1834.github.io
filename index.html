1. Kimchi Stew

  • Time: half hour
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
kimchi 2 cups
pork belly 1/2 cup
neutral oil 2 tbsp
sugar 1 tbsp
cubed tofu half pkg

Step 1: fry kimchi in the oil and sugar over med-high heat for 5-7 minutes.

Step 2: add water to liking and pork.

Step 3: bring to a boil, add tofu and serve with rice.

2. Seaweed Soup

  • Time: half hour
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
seaweed 2 cups
beef 1/2 cup
neutral oil 2 tbsp
garlic 2 tbsp
anchovy stock 4 cups

Step 1: fry seaweed and garlic in the oil and over med-high heat for 5-7 minutes.

Step 2: add stock and beef.

Step 3: bring to a boil, serve with rice.

3. Miso Soup

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
miso paste 3 tbsp
cubed tofu half pkg
green onion half cup
dashi stock 3 cups

Step 1: fry kimchi in the oil and sugar over med-high heat for 5-7 minutes.

Step 2: add water to liking and pork.

Step 3: bring to a boil, add tofu and serve with rice.
